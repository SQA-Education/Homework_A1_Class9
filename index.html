🎧 1. Listening — At the Market

Listen to the conversation and answer the questions.

Anna: Hi Lisa. Where do you buy your vegetables?

Lisa: I buy them at the farmer's market.

Anna: How much are the tomatoes there?

Lisa: They are 2 dollars a pound.

Anna: That's good! The supermarket sells them for 3 dollars.

Lisa: I also buy bread and eggs there.

Anna: How much are the eggs?

Lisa: They are 4 dollars a dozen. Very fresh!

1. Where does Lisa buy vegetables?

2. How much are the tomatoes?

3. What else does Lisa buy at the market?
